Fashion

and design

The Fashion Brand journey began in 1954 as a lingerie boutique in Alingsas, Sweden. From there, we have added womenswear, kids and men's clothing, cosmetics and accessories to our crisp, chic, contemporary collections. That lingerie boutique is now joined by nearly 500 stores offering the complete Fashion Brand collection around the world.

We are proud of our Scandinavian roots, and place great importance on quality

Production

The Fashion Brand production process is a global experience. We select high-quality raw materials, such as cotton grown under the Better Cotton Initiative in India.

We do not own any factories, and work with independent suppliers and factories around the world. Our on-site Fashion Brand staff works alongside local factory employees to ensure our designs are achieved under the best operating methods. On the factory floor, we begin by knitting the fabrics we use. The knitted fabric is then passed on to the dyeing station to create the unique Fashion Brand. The dyed fabric is formed as the finished garment begins to take shape. The craftsmanship involved in producing the maximum number of garments out of each piece of fabric is a true art and one that we value greatly.

From our head office in Gothenburg, Sweden, our extensive team of designers, purchasers, assistants, pattern designers, product managers, and purchase controllers bring ideas to life.
